SAFE Personal Trainer Kit

Febuary 2025 - April 2025

Worked with a team of Brandeis students to design and build an end-to-end workout analyzer that runs on a Raspberry Pi hub and pairs with a Raspberry Pi Pico over BLE for live metrics. The system performs on-device pose estimation to assess form and provide feedback that helps prevent injuries with the goal of making personal training accessible.

  • Python
  • Tkinter
  • OpenCV
  • Pillow
  • Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) with Bleak
  • TensorFlow Lite (MoveNet)
  • NumPy
  • Raspberry Pi
